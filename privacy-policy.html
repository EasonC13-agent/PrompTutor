Privacy Policy

Chat Collector for Research - Chrome Extension

Last updated: February 2025

Purpose

Chat Collector for Research is a Chrome extension developed for educational research on student-AI interactions. It is part of a research project at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). The study aims to understand how students use AI chatbots for learning, in order to improve AI-assisted education.

Data We Collect

When you explicitly opt in, the extension collects chat conversation text from supported AI platforms:

  • ChatGPT
  • Claude
  • Grok
  • Gemini
  • DeepSeek
  • Copilot
  • Perplexity
  • Poe
  • Doubao
  • HuggingChat

We do not collect browsing history, passwords, personal files, or any data outside of AI chat conversations on the listed platforms.

Opt-In Only

Data collection is strictly opt-in. The extension does not collect any data until you actively enable collection. You can toggle data collection on or off at any time through the extension popup.

Anonymization

All collected data is anonymized before storage. Personally identifiable information is removed so that conversation data cannot be traced back to individual users.

Data Storage and Security

Collected data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secure research servers. Access to the data is restricted to authorized members of the research team.

Your Controls

  • You can enable or disable data collection at any time.
  • You can view your collected data through the extension.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the research team.
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time to stop all activity.

Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is used solely for academic research purposes.

IRB Approval

This research is subject to review by the Carnegie Mellon University Institutional Review Board (IRB). IRB approval is pending.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
